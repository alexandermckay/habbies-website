    WHY BUY FROM US?

    Grain fed cattle account for over 50% of the cattle slaughtered in Australia each year. Grain fed cattle are often raised in conditions akin to a battery farmed chicken. Their movement is limited, their diet is simplistic - low in both fats and protein - and their quality of life is greatly diminished.

    Not only is this bad for the animal, it is bad for the consumer. Muscle from a primarily grain fed animal often lacks the omega-3s and essential minerals and vitamins that you would find in a grass fed equivalent.

    If you want the best for your customers, you should buy grass fed, paddock raised meat.

    WHAT DOES A FEEDLOT LOOK LIKE?

    A feedlot is just a giant patch of dirt where cattle are raised in unhealthy, unnatural conditions, usually requiring high doses of hormones, antibiotics and antiparasitics to produce beef. Cattle are born elsewhere on many different farms and moved into a single location. This spreads diseases and parasites as well as decreasing animal welfare and consequentially the quality of the beef.

    WHAT DOES A HABBIES FEEDLOT LOOK LIKE?

    A Habbies Howe feedlot doesn't exist. Instead we grow and fatten all of our cattle on a farm, in a paddock, that is stacked with grass. Cattle are never moved in from another farm. Every animal has spent its entire life at Habbies Howe. This allows us to have 100% traceability and highly refined genetics.
