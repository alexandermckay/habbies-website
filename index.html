    WHAT MAKES HABBIES HOWE BEEF SPECIAL?

    Situated at the southern end of the Strathbogie Ranges in the Victorian Highlands, Habbies Howe is unique. Fresh spring water and fertile soils enable us to grow grass fed, naturally marbled, mineral rich beef. Our cattle avoid unnatural hormones, feedlots, grains and stress, ensuring you receive high quality, tender and tasty beef.
