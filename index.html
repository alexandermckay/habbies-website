    WHY IS HABBIES HOWE BEEF WORTH IT?

    Our cattle thrive in the idyllic environment of the Strathbogie Highlands, nourished by crystal-clear spring water and rich, fertile soils. This natural setting allows us to raise grass-fed, mineral-rich beef with exceptional flavour and marbling.

    Grass fed

    Paddock raised

    Fertile soils

    We prioritise the well-being of our animals, ensuring they are free from artificial hormones, feedlots, and grains. The result? You enjoy tender and incredibly tasty beef.

    Low stress

    Stable climate

    Natural growth

    Don't just take our word for it. Each week we sell 2.5 tonnes of beef all over Victoria to butchers, restaurants and food producers. If you live in North East Victoria it is likely that you've already eaten Habbies Howe beef without realising it - you just thought you were eating fantastic beef.
